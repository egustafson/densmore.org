The Books

You may be wondering why books like Leading Change and Let's Get Real are on our lis of references, when they don't really deal with systems development, use cases or SOA. Our experience is that much of the battle is organizational change and selling the client on the value of the solution. Kotter's seminal work is the best we know about creating lasting and useful change in an organization. Khalsa's Let's Get Real is the only book that I've seen that truly informs its readers how to ensure a win-win sales situation in collaboration with your client.
