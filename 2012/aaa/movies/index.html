Movies

I've never done much in the way of making movies. Too hard. Until now; iPhone changes everything. These are as much about the sound as anything else. The iPhone picked up the sounds amazingly well for such an incredibly tiny box.

These quicktime movies were taken by me at the 2012 Antique Airplane Association fly-in at Antique Airfield (IA27) near Blakesburg, IA over Labor Day weekend.
