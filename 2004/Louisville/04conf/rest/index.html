2004 Conference USA Swimming Championships

University of Louisville

These photos are from the Friday and Saturday sessions at the 2004 Conference meet, emphasizing U of L's participation.

Something interesting happened on the way to the meet. I discovered I had forgotten my camera battery charger. It remained at home.

Therefore, I decided that on Friday I would take photos only during the finals session to ensure I had something left for Saturday. Sorry if I missed getting a shot of your favorite swimmer! On Saturday I found I was doing okay and once again became freer taking lots of photos.

I don't recall names too well. Sorry, haven't been to too many meets because I live in Colorado. So I'm afraid you'll have to peruse through some these pictures looking for your swimmer, hope that's ok.

Here are the particulars of the photos presented here.

  • All taken with a Canon EOS Digital Rebel, most with a 75-300mm zoom. (Unfortunately, with the light available, this is a tenuous way to work!)
  • If I had a blurred shot I still wanted to include because of its content value, I lowered its resolution to save disk space. Magnify these in order to see them full-size if necessary. Some are cropped as well to better capture the subject. The full-size photos are about 300K.
  • If I happened to get what I considered a really good quality shot -- in focus, good subject, interesting to the audience (your mileage may vary :) -- it is presented not only as a compressed JPEG but also in raw JPEG format (over 2MB in size) for highest resolution. These are marked [Large].
  • These photos are presented in chronological order, by column. They include photos from Friday and Saturday preliminaries and finals sessions.
