Here are some shots of Hank and Linda Fore's 185, taken by Erik Mugele from 55A while we were on our way to a breakfast meeting with other 180/185 owners. The date was 16 November 2002, another great Colorado day. Hank did a great job with the formation flying while I led. We used the only film I had lying around, 400 ASA print film from Kodak. The camera is my old but trusty and excellent Canon AE-1 with a 200mm zoom.

Would you like to see our airplane from Hank and Linda's? The Fores shot these photos at the same time.
